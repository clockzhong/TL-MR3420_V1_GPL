Certificate Management Help

You can generate new SSL/TLS certificate and private key for your router on this page.

  • Common Name - Common name for the new certificate.
  • Email Address - Email address to be embedded in the new certificate.

Note:

  1. Once you have clicked Save, the router will generate a new pair of certificate and private key, but they won't take effect until the router is rebooted.
  2. You may need to re-configure your browser after generating a new certificate, to ensure access to the web interface.
